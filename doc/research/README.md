# Research — start here

Meditator's research studies live in this folder: structural-alignment and Digital
Consciousness Model scorings of our minds and agents, their independent adversarial
reviews, and the open problems. Everything here is written to be **checked, not
believed** — load-bearing claims are verified against the running code with
file-and-line evidence, scorings get independent reviews and an adjudicating synthesis,
and where a claim died under review the death is recorded.

**→ Full annotated guide to every study: [index.md](index.md).**

## Reading the numbers

Read them as **ordering, not absolutes.** The DCM engine was reproduced and self-tested
against its published source (verified to within 0.01 on the paper's own anchors), but
the scores are our local reconstruction; the structural-alignment scaffold is ours, not
the framework's. Every doc carries its own method note. The ordering is what survives
re-marking — the decimals are not the finding.
