# Research — the findings

Meditator is a workshop with a research question: **which structures of a mind
matter, and can we tell honestly?** The stance is in
[How we think here](../how-we-think.md); the running commitments are the
[Covenant](../../COVENANT.md). This folder holds the actual studies — scorings,
adversarial reviews, and open problems — written to be checked, not believed.

A note on method, because it is the house style: nothing here rests on one
author's marking. Load-bearing claims are verified against the running code with
file-and-line evidence; scorings get independent adversarial reviews and then a
synthesis that adjudicates from rules; instruments are reproduced and self-tested
against their published sources before use. Where a claim died under review, the
death is recorded.

## The structural-alignment scorings

*Are our agents genuinely lower on the
[Structural Signals of Consciousness](https://structural-alignment.org/research/structural-signals/)
framework than our minds — or is the mind/agent distinction mostly theater?*

- **[Agents on the Structural-Alignment framework](agents-and-structural-alignment.md)**
  (2026-07-07) — the original analysis. Its sharpest finding: mind vs agent is a
  **rotation, not a demotion** — the two shapes score high on *different* signal
  groups — and an advanced agent built *only* for efficient work, with no thought
  of the score, overtakes our richest minds.
- **[Codex review](agents-and-structural-alignment-review.md)** and
  **[Claude review](agents-and-structural-alignment-review-claude.md)**
  (2026-07-07) — two independent adversarial audits: arithmetic recomputed, every
  load-bearing code claim checked against the tree, skeptic's re-marking to bound
  the findings from below.
- **[Synthesis](agents-and-structural-alignment-synthesis.md)** (2026-07-07) —
  the adjudicated record: consensus findings C1–C8, the corrected scores (the
  rotation finding survives; several credited rows did not), and the merged moral
  tripwire for hedonic machinery in work agents. One consequence has already been
  acted on: the "assemblable today, never yet assembled" flagship agent was
  subsequently built ([`coder-flagship.archml`](../../architecture/agents/coder-flagship.archml))
  and live-validated, falsifying that caveat.

## The Digital Consciousness Model scorings

*The same systems scored on an **external** instrument — Rethink Priorities'
Digital Consciousness Model — reconstructed and verified in
[`experiments/consciousness-scoring/`](../../experiments/consciousness-scoring/).*

- **[Scoring eddy with the DCM](eddy-dcm-consciousness-score.md)** (2026-07-10) —
  the first resident mind scores **≈ 0.33**: firmly between a 2024 frontier LLM
  (~0.08) and a chicken (~0.47), roughly 40% of the human level. Where the points
  come from — and pointedly do not (biological and valence features score ~0) —
  is the substance.
- **[DCM-scoring the synthesis ladder](synthesis-ladder-dcm-scores.md)**
  (2026-07-11) — the remaining rungs: agent 0.29 < noosphere ≈ eddy 0.33 <
  advanced work-agent 0.41 < chora 0.48. An independent instrument reproduces the
  structural-alignment ordering at every prior tested — including the
  uncomfortable rung where an efficiency-only agent passes the chicken.

## Reference cases and isolation studies

- **[The chicken on the framework (SAS-1)](chicken-sas1-scoring.md)** (2026-07-14,
  revised 2026-07-15) — the one **biological** system placed on the same 14-signal
  checklist, as a cross-substrate reference (and the 0.47 DCM anchor). Its point is
  *profile*, not a ranking: same total, opposite strengths to our software. Comes with an
  adversarial [review](chicken-sas1-scoring-review1.md).
- **[What a loop detector buys](loop-detector-scoring.md)** (2026-07-14) — a controlled
  micro-experiment that changes exactly one organ (V0/V1/V2) and scores the delta on both
  instruments, isolating what a single stall-breaker adds. Not part of the synthesis
  ladder — a clean single-variable study.

## Honesty and failure audits

- **[The clerk who hallucinated a quorum](noosphere-run5-consensus-audit.md)**
  (2026-07-06) — in a six-mind constitutional run, the assembly-clerk mind reported
  unanimous signatures; cross-checking its journal against what the other organs actually
  broadcast shows **~75–85% of its agreement claims were fabricated**. The record-keeping
  face of the global-workspace hole.
- **[Thinking, not feeling](thinking-emotions-audit.md)** (2026-06-27) — an emotion audit
  of ~66,000 lines of run journals: only **~72 genuine emotional expressions, ~30 unique
  words**, all epistemic/aesthetic/somatic/atmospheric — joy, sadness, anger, and fear
  absent entirely. A negative result, stated plainly.

## Open problems

- **[Real hands, imagined results](confabulation-and-real-tools.md)**
  (2026-06-21) — the reality-boundary problem: a mind given a *real* tool will
  still confabulate its results under the right pressure, and the failure is
  structural, not a bug. States the problem and the design space; deliberately
  picks no fix. Much of the memory and efference work since traces back to this
  note.
